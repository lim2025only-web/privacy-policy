Privacy Policy

Last updated: 31 August 2025

App Name: Tech VA Operator: John Paul Lim Contact: Lim2025only@gmail.com

We respect your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect when you use this app and related services (including Facebook integrations like Login, Pages, and Messenger), how we use it, and your rights.

1) Information We Collect

  • Account & Profile Data: name, email, profile ID, and other information you choose to share via Facebook permissions you approve.
  • Page & Messenger Data: messages you send to the Page, message metadata (timestamps, sender/recipient IDs), attachments you send (images/documents), delivery/read events, and webhook updates necessary to operate automations.
  • Technical Data: IP address, device/browser type, locale, and logs generated by our servers to ensure security and reliability.

2) How We Use Information

  • Provide core features (e.g., respond to messages, automate replies, process attachments for the requested service such as printing).
  • Operate webhooks and integrations with Facebook products according to the permissions you grant.
  • Improve reliability, prevent abuse, debug issues, and comply with legal obligations.
  • Communicate with you about service updates and support.

3) Legal Basis

If you are in the EEA/UK, we process data based on your consent (when granting Facebook permissions), performance of a contract (to deliver requested services), and legitimate interests (to keep services secure and functional).

4) Sharing & Disclosure

We do not sell your personal information. We may share limited data with service providers who help us operate the app (e.g., hosting, storage, analytics) under confidentiality obligations, and when required by law or to protect rights, safety, and security.

5) Data Retention

We keep personal information only as long as necessary for the purposes described above, then delete or anonymize it. For Messenger/Page data, retention generally mirrors your conversation history unless you request deletion sooner.

6) Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards to protect your data. However, no system is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

7) Your Rights

  • Access, correct, or delete your personal information.
  • Withdraw consent for optional processing where applicable.
  • Object to or restrict certain processing and request portability, as provided by law.

To exercise these rights, contact us at Lim2025only@gmail.com.

8) Facebook Data & Permissions

When you connect Facebook products, the app accesses data strictly according to the permissions you approve (e.g., pages_messaging, pages_show_list, pages_manage_metadata). We use this data only to provide requested features and never for unrelated purposes.

9) Webhooks

Our app may receive Facebook Webhook events to deliver real‑time functionality (e.g., new message notifications). These events are processed securely on our servers and logged for reliability and troubleshooting.

10) Children

This service is not directed to children under 13 (or the minimum age required in your country). We do not knowingly collect information from children.

11) International Transfers

Your information may be processed and stored in countries other than your own. Where required, we use appropriate safeguards to permit such transfers.

12) Data Deletion

You can request deletion of your data at any time by emailing Lim2025only@gmail.com with the subject Data Deletion Request and your Facebook User ID or Page ID. If you interacted via Messenger, you may also delete the conversation thread on Facebook to remove message history on your side.

Developer note for Facebook App Review: If you host this page on your domain, you may also expose a simple endpoint like POST /data-deletion to handle automated deletion requests and reference it here.

13) Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will change the “Last updated” date above and, where appropriate, provide additional notice.
